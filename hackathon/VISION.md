# Startup Vision

## Mission
**Ensure no one dies alone because they could not reach help.**

## Vision (10-year)
Lifeline AI becomes the **trusted national emergency layer** — integrated with 999/112, hospital triage, and municipal risk planning — so every citizen, from Lagos to rural Wales, has the same seconds-to-help standard.

## Why now
- Smartphone penetration + edge AI enable on-device detection without cloud latency
- Climate and urbanization increase disaster and corridor risk
- Governments seek **digital sovereignty** in public safety tech
- Telcos and insurers want embedded life-safety products

## Principles
1. **Offline is not optional** — infrastructure must work in dead zones
2. **Dispatchers are users too** — UX for EMS equals UX for victims
3. **Predict, don't just react** — move safety upstream with Danger Zone AI
4. **Privacy by design** — minimum data, role-based access, auditable trails

## North-star metric
**Median time from distress signal to verified responder dispatch** — target 30% reduction in pilot corridors within 12 months.

## Tagline
*Next-generation national emergency response infrastructure.*
