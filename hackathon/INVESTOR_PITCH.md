# Investor Pitch Structure

## 1. Vision
**Become the national emergency response operating system** — every citizen protected, every dispatcher empowered, every route made safer by AI.

## 2. Problem
- **Access:** 1.5B+ people in low-connectivity regions cannot reliably reach emergency services.
- **Speed:** Urban EMS averages 8–12 min; rural can exceed 45 min.
- **Intelligence gap:** Dispatchers lack predictive risk; victims lack situational awareness.

## 3. Solution — Lifeline AI Platform
| Layer | Product | Value |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Citizen | Flutter mobile app | SOS, AI triggers, offline, contacts |
| Intelligence | Danger Zone Engine | Hotspots, heatmaps, route safety |
| Operations | Command Dashboard | Live map, dispatch, analytics |
| Infrastructure | Node API + Firebase + WebSocket | Real-time national backbone |

## 4. Traction (hackathon / pilot framing)
- End-to-end MVP: mobile + API + dashboard + demo simulation
- Offline module + motion AI + safety engine implemented
- Demo-ready for municipalities and hackathon judges

## 5. Market
- **TAM:** Global public safety & emergency comms ~$50B+
- **SAM:** National EMS dispatch software + citizen safety apps
- **SOM:** Pilot city (1–5M population) → regional expansion

## 6. Business model
| Stream | Model |
|--------|--------|
| Government / EMS | Annual SaaS per dispatcher seat + incident volume tier |
| Enterprise | Campus, mining, logistics — private command centers |
| Consumer | Freemium SOS; premium family plans + insurance partnerships |
| Data (ethical) | Aggregated anonymized safety insights for urban planning |

## 7. Go-to-market
1. Hackathon + university pilots
2. Single-city EMS partnership (90-day pilot)
3. Ministry of interior / health RFP alignment
4. Insurance & telco distribution (pre-installed SOS)

## 8. Team ask
- Technical founders + advisory EMS/medical
- **Raising:** Pre-seed for pilot deployment, compliance, 24/7 ops runbook

## 9. Use of funds
- 40% engineering (mobile hardening, mesh production)
- 30% pilot ops & integrations (CAD, 999, hospital APIs)
- 20% compliance & security
- 10% GTM

## 10. Closing
> Lifeline AI turns fragmented panic buttons into **coordinated national infrastructure** — saving minutes, saving lives.
