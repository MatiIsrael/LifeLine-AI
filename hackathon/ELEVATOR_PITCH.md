# Elevator Pitch (60 seconds)

**Lifeline AI is the operating system for national emergency response.**

When someone falls in a field with no signal, presses SOS in a dangerous neighborhood, or can't speak during a crime — Lifeline gets help there anyway. Our mobile app uses **on-device AI** to detect falls, crashes, and panic; **offline SMS and mesh relay** when the internet fails; and **one-tap SOS** with live GPS and trusted contacts.

On the other side, **Lifeline Command** gives hospitals, police, and EMS a real-time map — WebSocket incidents, dispatch, and route optimization. Our **Danger Zone AI** predicts crime and accident hotspots so victims and responders see risk before it happens.

We're not another panic button. We're **infrastructure**: victim layer, intelligence layer, responder layer — built for cities and rural dead zones alike.

**We're raising pilot partnerships with cities and EMS networks to prove 30% faster time-to-dispatch in our first corridor.**
